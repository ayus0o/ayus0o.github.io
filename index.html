About the Artist

My practice begins with observation — the quality of morning light crossing a windowsill, the weight of a figure held in the memory of charcoal on paper. I work slowly, returning to each piece over days and weeks, building layers that accumulate like sediment: transparent washes, graphite marks, moments of stillness.

I trained at the Royal College of Art and have exhibited across Europe and North America. My work is held in private and institutional collections in the United Kingdom, Germany, and the United States. I live and work in London.

Available for commissions, exhibitions, and institutional collaborations.
